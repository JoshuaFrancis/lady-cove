# The Lady Cove — Content Strategy

## Content Pillars

### 1. Rare Plant Care Expertise
**Target audience**: Collectors and intermediate growers
**Content types**: Genus care guides, propagation tips, troubleshooting
**SEO play**: Long-tail "[genus] care guide" keywords (e.g., "anthurium care guide", "alocasia care guide")
**Existing assets**: 17 detailed care guides already written on current Shopify site

### 2. Live-Selling Community
**Target audience**: Palmstreet users, plant auction enthusiasts
**Content types**: How-to-buy guides, livestream schedules, community highlights
**SEO play**: "buy rare plants online", "palmstreet plant seller", "live plant auctions"
**Existing assets**: Twice-daily livestream content, community testimonials

### 3. Plant Subscriptions & Collecting
**Target audience**: Collectors wanting curated monthly boxes
**Content types**: Subscription box descriptions, unboxing content, collector guides
**SEO play**: "rare plant subscription box", "monthly plant box", "aroid subscription"
**Existing assets**: Deep knowledge of genus families for curation

### 4. Brand Story & Trust
**Target audience**: New visitors evaluating whether to buy
**Content types**: Origin story, packing quality proof, certifications, reviews
**SEO play**: "the lady cove reviews", "the lady cove plants", brand searches
**Existing assets**: Etsy reviews, Facebook recommendations, founder story
